About Us

Welcome to Fries Lab – Where Flavor Meets Creativity!

Founded in 2022 by food enthusiast Ahmad Wehbe, Fries Lab was born out of a passion for bold flavors and the love of Lebanese street food. Located in the heart of Beirut, we specialize in loaded fries, juicy burgers, and mouthwatering wraps — all made with fresh, locally sourced potatoes and high-quality ingredients.

From crispy golden fries drenched in signature sauces to our unforgettable boneless chicken wraps, every item on our menu is crafted to satisfy your cravings and ignite your taste buds.

Despite the challenges faced, including surviving the 2024 conflict, Fries Lab has remained committed to delivering comfort food with a twist. Whether you’re dining in or ordering out, we promise fast service, generous portions, and unforgettable taste.

Join us, and discover why Fries Lab is one of Beirut’s favorite street food spots!
